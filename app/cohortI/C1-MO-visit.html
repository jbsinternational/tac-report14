Strengths and Considerations for Action

Program Vision and Design

Strengths

  • Mobile MET has a comprehensive and advanced electronic health record (EHR) called Community Care Linke (CCL) that electronically links 26 agencies. Since development of the CCL platform, the COMBAT-funded agencies have established service records for over 13,000 clients.
  • Mobile MET is user-friendly and has the potential to expand and be utilized by most agencies linked to the COMBAT connections interface.
  • The Web-based client portal/EHR can aid efforts of participating agencies to be successful in participating in local efforts to adapt systems to the challenges and opportunities inherent in health care reform, particularly given their ability to connect to the health information exchange (HIE).
