Population Served

  • Rural college students with substance abuse disorders

Project Goals

  • Expand evidence-supported substance abuse treatment services to rural college students via telemedicine.
  • Increase care coordination by using telemedicine to link rural students, families, campus health personnel, the substance abuse team, and the mental health specialty team.
  • Use Web-based technologies to enhance peer-to-peer substance abuse prevention strategies at rural college campuses.
