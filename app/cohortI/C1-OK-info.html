Population Served

  • Rural clients living in six counties in south central Oklahoma (Hughes, Northeastern Pontotoc, Okfuskee, Seminole, Southern Potawatomi, and Southern Cleveland)

Project Goals

  • Leverage technology to enhance the capacity of COFMC to serve the rural population in the six counties of its service area.
  • Integrate Web-based services, the NextGen (electronic health record system), and other technological advances (e.g., text and instant messaging, teleconferencing via Web platforms) to increase treatment and intervention opportunities for clients seeking treatment for substance abuse problems.
