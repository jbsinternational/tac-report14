Population Served

  • Individuals with a substance use disorder living in rural regions

Project Goals

  • Human Service Center’s goals are to: 1) allow individuals with substance use disorders living in rural regions access to a range of e-therapy options, and 2) combine treatment with community-based clinical services and access to medical care based on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and recovery-based resources. Specifically, Human Service Center strives to—
    • Develop an expedited screening and referral protocol for rural clients
    • Implement Web and phone-based technologies for addiction treatment interventions to deliver CBT skills training
    • Develop a series of protocols that keep individuals engaged in CBT skills training and relapse prevention for at least 6 months through the use of multiple e-therapy options
