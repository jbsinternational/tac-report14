Project Goals

  • The IRHIT program intends to 1) increase treatment capacity by providing opportunities for clients to complete anonymous online assessments, 2) expand access to educational materials, and 3) enhance intensive outpatient treatment and recovery services. Specifically:
    • Contracted providers will increase the number of rural adults assessed and retained in intensive outpatient and recovery services by utilizing expanded health information technology.
    • IDPH-funded providers will expand and enhance substance abuse treatment services offered by agencies by implementing a planned IRHIT project.
    • IDPH will implement and sustain diffusion of enhanced health IT to the 23 IDPH-funded substance abuse treatment agencies (9 agencies in year 1 and 7 agencies in years 2 and 3, respectively).
