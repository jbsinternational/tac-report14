Population Served

Project Goals

  • Promote high-quality health care and wellness through a trusting and respectful manner to the individual and community while maintaining the traditional way for Native Americans.
  • Increase and enhance the delivery of rural mental health services through the use of technology, regardless of rural geographic proximity.
  • Use telehealth to promote wellness and patient engagement.
  • Empower patients through integrated outreach services, programs, and overall wellness from the diverse departments available at the Health & Wellness Center (HWC).
