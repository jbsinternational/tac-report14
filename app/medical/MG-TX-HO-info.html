Goals

  • Train all medical residents in family medicine, pediatrics, general internal medicine, psychiatry, and trauma in SBIRT, utilizing the evidence-based methods approach developed in the InSight Project.
  • Train selected faculty and resident champions within each residency program in how to teach SBIRT services using a train-the-trainer model.
  • Expand InSight SBIRT training and services to MEDVAMC and TCH.
  • Develop enduring training materials that can be disseminated through Web-based methods.

Population Served

  • BCM residents provide services to patients in HCHD, MEDVAMC, and TCH. HCHD is the fourth largest public health care system in the United States, and MEDVAMC is the primary area home for 120,000 veterans.
  • Patients are primarily from Harris County but include people from surrounding counties and States.
  • The patient population includes people aged 10 and up from diverse ethnic and racial backgrounds.
  • English, Spanish, and Vietnamese are three major languages spoken in the area.
