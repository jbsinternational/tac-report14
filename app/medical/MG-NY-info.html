Goals

  • Train 500 residents over 5 years using a faculty mentor model.
  • Develop multiple online modules to include in the curriculum.
  • Develop specialty-specific training programs that include demonstrations, videos, role plays, standardized patients, resident information packets, mock scripts, and articles.
  • Launch an SBIRT Web site.

Implementation Sites

  • Albany Medical Center Psychiatry Outpatient Clinic
  • Internal Medicine Outpatient Clinic

Population Served

  • Albany Medical Center is the only academic health sciences center in the 25 counties of eastern New York and Western New England, serving 2.8 million residents.
  • Compared to other area hospitals, Albany Medical Center provides a disproportionate amount of care to the underserved, carrying nearly 40 percent of Medicaid and self-pay residents.
