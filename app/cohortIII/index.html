Funded in 2011 and 2012

State Grantees, Cohort III, implement SBIRT services for adults in primary care and community health settings. This program is designed to expand and enhance the State and tribal continuum-of-care services for substance misuse, reduce alcohol and drug consumption and their negative health impact, reduce costly health care utilization, and promote sustainability and behavioral health information technology.
