Project Goals

  • Deploy a clinical team in a technology equipped van in rural, eastern NC to conduct mobile outreach to veterans who are homeless or under-served.
  • Coordinate services with the Veterans Affairs homeless and rural health programs and community-based treatment providers and resources.
  • Provide state-of-the-art, evidence-based interventions via telehealth, web- and application-based service delivery systems.
  • Share the practices and the results of our project with providers in rural, eastern NC.
