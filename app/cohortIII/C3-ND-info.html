Project Goals

  • Enhance access, engagement, retention in treatment, continuing care services, and postdischarge care using technology-assisted care for up to 60 adults per year, aged 18 and over, who reside in rural and underserved areas and/or have special needs including Native Americans and individuals with opioid addiction, psychiatric comorbidity, and/or co-occurring health issues.
  • Improve the rates of participation and completion of continuing care from 40 to 60 percent.
  • Improve lifestyles and decisionmaking abilities regarding mental health and health care.
