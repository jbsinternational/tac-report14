Population Served

  • RCCCP’s Client Access and Services Exchange (CASE) project recruit and engage adults (aged 18 or older) who are uninsured and underinsured living in central Missouri’s Randolph, Saline, Pettis, Lafayette, Boone, and Cooper counties and who are seeking pretreatment, treatment, and recovery services.

Project Goals

  • Share effective treatment models and results among providers.
  • Increase engagement of persons in treatment for health care.
  • Increase monitoring and tracking the health status of individuals.
  • Improve recovery and resiliency rates.
  • Increase intrinsic motivation to change.
  • Increase retention among pretreatment clients who used technology-based services.
  • Increase retention for clients who used technology-based services from treatment admission to recovery.
