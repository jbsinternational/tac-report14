Population Served

  • The population of Trenton—the target for initial implementation—is young relative to the overall county and State population, with 57 percent of residents in the 25–44-year age bracket and approximately 25 percent under 18 years of age.
  • The community is highly diverse—46 percent are African American, 19 percent are Hispanic, and 40 percent are White. The unemployment rate is high at 9 percent, and 37 percent of the community live below the Federal poverty level.
  • Trenton has high rates of substance abuse and crime.
