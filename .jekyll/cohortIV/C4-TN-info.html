Goals

  • Implement SBIRT in at least six health centers that will provide routine screening of all patients in these primary care settings.
  • Achieve SBIRT dissemination statewide within primary care systems.

Implementation Sites

ETSU Family Medicine Associates of Johnson City

The Primary Care Clinic at Nashville General Hospital (Meharry)

ETSU Family Medicine Clinic-Bristol

Tennessee Army National Guard (TN ANG) Armory, Milan

ETSU Family Medicine Clinic

TN ANG Armory, Smyrna

Population Served

Two distinct populations are served through this initiative. Rural, Appalachian poor patients who are often difficult to reach are served through the ETSU site clinics. In addition, a highly diverse population of urban poor is served at site clinics through Meharry Medical College, an historically Black institution.
