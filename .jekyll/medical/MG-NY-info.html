SBIRT-Medical Residency Program
Albany Medical Center

  • Program Information
  • Site Visit Report

Location:
Albany, New York

TI Number:
TI020269

Funding Period:
09/30/08–09/29/13

Grantee Project Director:
Victoria Balkoski, M.D.
Tel: 518-262-2466
Email: balkosv@mail.amc.edu

Government Project Officer:
Erich Kleinschmidt, M.S.W., LICSW

Partnering Organizations

  • Albany Medical Center
  • Albany Medical College
  • New York State Office of Alcoholism and Substance Abuse Services
  • New York State SBIRT
  • Healthy Capital District Initiative
  • Albany County Department of Mental Health Community Assessment and Referral Team

Goals

  • Train 500 residents over 5 years using a faculty mentor model.
  • Develop multiple online modules to include in the curriculum.
  • Develop specialty-specific training programs that include demonstrations, videos, role plays, standardized patients, resident information packets, mock scripts, and articles.
  • Launch an SBIRT Web site.

Implementation Sites

  • Albany Medical Center Psychiatry Outpatient Clinic
  • Internal Medicine Outpatient Clinic

Population Served

  • Albany Medical Center is the only academic health sciences center in the 25 counties of eastern New York and Western New England, serving 2.8 million residents.
  • Compared to other area hospitals, Albany Medical Center provides a disproportionate amount of care to the underserved, carrying nearly 40 percent of Medicaid and self-pay residents.

TRAINEE POPULATIONS

  • Residents from numerous specialty areas
    • Psychiatry
    • Internal medicine
    • Pediatrics
    • Combined medicine/pediatrics
    • Family medicine
    • Obstetrics/gynecology
    • Emergency medicine
  • Psychology interns, postdoctoral fellows, and practicum students
  • Medical students during third year psychiatry clerkship
  • Social workers and social work student interns
  • Nurse practitioners
  • Psychiatry faculty
