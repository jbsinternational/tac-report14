Medical Residency Grantees

Funded in 2008 and 2009

Medical Residency Grantees develop and implement training programs to teach medical residents and allied health professionals the knowledge and skills to provide evidence-based SBIRT for patients at risk for SUDs who are in primary care and in various medical specialties. They also train local and statewide medical communities for wider dissemination of SBIRT practices. For more information, click on the 17 sites in the map.
