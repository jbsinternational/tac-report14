Medical Professional Training Grantees

Funded in 2013

Training Grantees develop and implement programs to teach health professionals (medical residents and students of nursing, social work, and counseling) the skills necessary to provide evidence-based SBIRT services to patients at risk for a substance use disorder. These programs teach health professionals in both didactic and practice settings and aim to increase the adoption and practice of SBIRT throughout the health care delivery system For more information, click on the 14 sites in the map.
